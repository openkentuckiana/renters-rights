Get help with your rental unit.

If you live in Louisville, Renter Haven can help you get help when you're having problems with your rental unit. We will help you find problems that your landlord is required by law to fix, and we will help you submit these problems to the right city agency.

Rules your landlord must follow

Your landlord must follow rules set by the City of Louisville. These rules exist to make sure rental properties are safe and healthy places to live.

We can show you the rules your landlord should be following and help you get help if they are not.

See the rules your landlord must follow

Getting help

If your landlord isn't following the rules, we can help you report violations to the city department that enforces the rules, Codes & Regulations.
